All objects in the universe are made of matter.
Matter can be detected and measured because it takes up space and it contains certain amount of material.
Matter can be identify or classify by characteristics called properties.
There are two kinds of properties and they are physical or chemical.
Another property of matter is energy but it can't be seen or touch, but all matter has it.
Energy can be transferred from one object to another but energy cannot be created or destroyed.
The most common energy is called mechanical energy.
There are two kinds of mechanical energy and they  kinetic and potential.
