Matter is the substance in which physical objects are composed of.
The classification of matter is identified by the chemical and physical properties of the object.
A physical property of a substances is the alteration of shape, mass and, etc.
The chemical property of matter is the complete change of a substances identity.
Energy is a scalar physical quantity that a property of objects and system of objects.
The law of conservation of energy state energy is neither created or destroyed, but constantly transferring.
Mechanical energy is the energy of an objects by it's position and motion.
The potential energy is a function of the position of an object.
The kinetic energy is a function of the objects movement.
