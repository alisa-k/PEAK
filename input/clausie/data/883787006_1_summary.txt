Everything in the universe is made of matter.
Matter can be detected and measured by its volume and mass.
Depends on the shape of matter some matter types can bee seen easy.
The other may be a little difficult to observe with a naked eye.
All matter has physical and chemical properties.
Physical properties do not have an ability to change matter to new kind of matter.
Most common physical properties of matter are density, malleability, boiling point, solubility.
Chemical properties of matter able to change matter to completely new form or kind of matter with different properties.
As a physical object matter has kinetic and potential energy.
Energy is a property of matter and can not be destroyed or created.
