Matter is the physical substance of a thing.
Matter is the stuff that all objects and substances in the universe are made of.
All matter takes up space, which is volume and contains a certain amount of material, which has mass.
Properties help us to identify and classify matter.
Physical change the shape and size can be alter, but not its chemical properties.
Chemical change the properties change and a new substance is produced.
Energy is a property of matter, and all matter has it.
Energy can be transferred from one object or system to another, however energy cannot be created or ever destroyed.
Mechanical energy is the energy an object has because of its motion or position.
