Matter is what makes up the universe.
It has shape, volume, and mass.
The properties of matter such as physical and chemical proeprties is what helps us classify matter.
Physical property is observed without changing the makeup or identity.
Chemical property is based on abilities to change into a new kind of matter.
Energy is in every matter.
All matter has potential or kinetic energy.
Potential energy is the energy in position or shape.
Kinetic energy is the energy matter has because its moving.
Energy can be transferred but never created or destroyed, according to the law of conservation.
