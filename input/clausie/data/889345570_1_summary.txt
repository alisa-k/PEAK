Matter is the stuff that substance and objects in the universe is made of.
All matter can be detected and measured, because matter takes up space and contains certain amount of material.
To classify matter you can easily observe it with your senses.
To classify other type of matter you may need a microscope to observe them.
The changes that matter has are physical change and chemical changes.
Physical change has size and shape.
Chemical change produces new substance.
Matter also has energy, which energy is the property of matter and all matter has it.
Energy has a law of conservation, which is diff.
forms transfer from one object or system.
There is a mechanical energy which is the energy of an object because of its motion or position.
