Matter makes up everything in the universe that takes up space and has mass.
Matter has many different properties such as density, malleability, and stability.
Matter can change physically and still be the same type, but it can also change chemically into different matter.
Energy is a properties of matter and all matter has it.
Law of conservation of energy states that energy can be transferred but not destroyed or created.
There are 3 types of energy: mechanical; the energy an object has because its poisition or motion, kinetic; energy because an object is moving, and potential; energy object has because of its position or shape.
