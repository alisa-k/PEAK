What is matter?

Matter is the "stuff" that makes up our universe.
Everything is made of matter.
Since matter is such a broad term scientists use different ways to identify or classify matter called properties.
There are physical and chemical properties as well as changes.
Energy is also a property of matter.
Energy comes in many different forms or stages.
Any action taken in the world required some form of energy whether it is kinetic, potential, or some other unnatural form.
