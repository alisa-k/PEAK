Matter is the "stuff" that all objects and substances in the universe are made of.
It can be detected by smelling it, seeing it, and feeling it.
It can also be measured because all matter takes up space and contains certain amount of materials.
Matter can be classify by properties there are 2 different properties: physical, and chemical properties.
Physical are those that can be observed, and chemical is based on its ability to change into a new kind of matter with different properties.
Energy can be related to matter, because energy is a property of matter.
Energy comes from 1 object system or another, energy cannot be created or destroyed.
This rule is known as the law of conservation of energy.
Another form of energy is mechanical energy, the energy of an object has of its motion or position.
