Matter makes up every object in the universe.
All matter has volume and mass, this is why it can be detected and measured.
All of our senses help us to classify matter in different ways.
All matter has physical and chemical properties these properties also help us classify matter.
Along with physical and chemical properties matter has physical and chemical changes physical change is when the objects shape changes and chemical change is when the object changes into a new substance.
Another main characteristic of matter is it all contains energy.
Energy is a property of matter.
Energy can be transferred from one object to another but cant be created or destroyed.
This is called the law of conservation of energy mechanical energy is one of the main types.
Mechanical energy is the energy it contains due to its motion or position.
