Matter is the stuff that all objects and substances in the universe are made of.
Matter has mass, volume and can be detected and measured.
Some types of matter are difficult to detect.
Matter has properties, they are called properties because they identify or classify matter.
All matter has both physical properties and chemical properties.
Physical properties are the ones that can be observed without changing the make-up, or identity of the matter.
Characteristc properties describe matter based on its ability to change into a new kind of matter with different properties.
Matter also has physical and chemical change.
