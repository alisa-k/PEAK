Matter is the stuff that all objects in our universe is made of.
All matter has mass and volume and with this being said, all matter can be measured and detected.
Matter also has different properties that make up certain objects such as physical and chemical.
Physical meaning that you can observe the substance without changing it's properties whereas chemical you can change the substance into a new property that isn't like the last.
The engine of matter is energy which has many different kinds such as mechanical, potential, and kinetic energy.
