Matter is what everthing is made of.
It has shape and volume.
Matter has properties that let us identify what it is.
These properties are divided in two, physical and chemical.
Because of these, matter goes through physical and chemical changes.
Another property of matter is energy.
There are mechanical energy, kinetic energy and potential energy.
Energy cannot be created or destroyed.
That is the law of conservation of energy.
