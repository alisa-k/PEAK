Matter is anything that has weight and occupies space.
It is classified by it's properties.
Physical properties are those that are observable and chemical ones are the reaction that take place between two matters.
Energy is a property of matter and has two types.
The law of conservation says it cannot be created or destroyed.
One type of energy is mechanical and it occurs according to motion or position of matter.
It comes in two types kinetic energy which is according to the movement and potential energy which is according to the body position of the matter.
