Matter is what makes all the objects and substances in the universe.
All matter have measurement and can be detected.
All matter can be detected because it takes up space and contains certain amount of material.
Matter can be classified easily with its.
Texture, shape, or weight.
All matter have physical and chemical properties.
Physical change is when an object shape changes physically.
Chemical change is when a chemical property of an object changes to a new substance.
Energy is a property of matter as well.
All matter has energy.
Energy cannot be created or destroyed, this is called the law of conservation of energy.
Mechanical energy is the energy an object has because its motion or position.
