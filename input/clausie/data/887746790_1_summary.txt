This passage talked about matter, the differences between physical and chemical changes, how energy is related to matter, the law of conservation of energy, and mechanical energy.
Matter is what everything in the universe is made of, it has volume, shape, and mass.
You can determine the different classifications of matter by its, physical, chemical, or energy structure.
Physical changes change the makeup or identity of matter, while chemical changes changes are based on abilities to change into a new kind of matter.
Matter has two types of energies.
Kinetic and potential.
The law of conservation of energy is that it can be transferred but not created or destroyed.
Last mechanical energy is what an object has in motion or position.
