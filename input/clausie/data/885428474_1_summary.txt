All objets and substances are made of matter.
Matter has volume and mass, so it can be detected and measured.
Physical properties produce physical changes.
They can be observed without changing the make-up of the matter.
Chemical properties produce chemical changes.
They create new substances.
Energy is another property of matter.
It can be transferred from one object to another.
It cannot be created or destroyed.
Mechanic energy has kinetic and potential energy.
Kinetic is the energy of a moving object.
Potential is the energy of the position or shape of an object.
