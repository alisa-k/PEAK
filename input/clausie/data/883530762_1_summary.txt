Matter is anyting that takes up space.
You can identify by using your senses.
If you can see, feel, or smell it, than it is matter.
A physical property is something that does not change its identity, and a chemical property can change into many different substances.
Energy is related to matter because energy is one of its properties.
Energy is not man made and it cant be destroyed.
Mechanical energy is energy an object has because of its position or motion.
When an object moves, it forms kinetic energy.
When you stretch a rubberband it forms potential energy.
