The paragraphs talk about matter and what is related to it.
Matter is everything around us, it has different forms and it comes in different properties as well.
Matter has two properties, physical and chemical.
Although they are different types of properties, they can be compared and stated that they can be related to each other.
Matter also has energy and it comes in different forms as well.
There are three types of energy in matter.
It is mechanical, kinetic, and potential energy.
Mechanical energy is the energy an object has because of its movement/motion or position.
Kinetic energy is the energy an object has because its moving.
Potential energy is the energy an object has because of its position or shape.
The law of conservation of energy states that energy cannot be created or destroyed.
